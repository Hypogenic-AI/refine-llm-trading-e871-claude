## Papers Directly Relevant to Trading Horizon Hypothesis

1. **FINSABER: Can LLM-based Financial Investing Strategies Outperform the Market in Long Run?** (2505.07078v4)
   - Authors: Li, Kim, Cucuringu, Ma (KDD 2026)
   - File: `2505.07078v4_can_llmbased_financial_investing_strategies_outperform.pdf`
   - Why relevant: 20-year backtest showing LLM trading advantages vanish under long-term evaluation; identifies regime-awareness as the key missing capability

2. **FinPos: A Position-Aware Trading Agent System** (2510.27251v2)
   - Authors: Liu, Dang
   - File: `2510.27251v2_finpos_a_positionaware_trading_agent_system.pdf`
   - Why relevant: First position-aware LLM agent with multi-timescale rewards (1/7/30-day); shows long-term trading dramatically improves LLM performance
